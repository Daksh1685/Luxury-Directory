Frequently Asked Questions

How does the vendor selection process work?

Our team carefully reviews each application to ensure it meets our quality standards. We prioritize businesses with established reputations for excellence in luxury services and may request additional information or verification during this process.

What are the benefits of being listed in the Luxury Directory?

Being listed provides exposure to our global network of high-net-worth individuals actively seeking luxury services. You'll receive premium placement, detailed service profiles, direct booking inquiries, and inclusion in our exclusive marketing campaigns.

Is there a fee to be listed in the directory?

Yes, we offer different membership tiers with varying benefits. After your application is approved, our team will contact you to discuss pricing options that best suit your business needs and service offering.

How long does the approval process take?

The typical review process takes 48-72 hours. Once approved, we'll work with you to create an optimized listing that highlights the unique aspects of your luxury service offering.
